About Me

Hi! I’m Kian a student with a deep love for creativity and self-expression. I’ve always been drawn to the worlds of coding, gaming, and art, and I enjoy building things that feel meaningful, whether it’s a cool website or a fun personal project. One of my biggest passions is gaming I get completely immersed in the worlds and stories they tell. I’m especially a huge fan of Expedition 33 and the Fate series. The characters and lore from those games really fuel my imagination and give me ideas for future projects. I also love listening to Ado’s music her voice is so powerful and unique. Her song Ibara especially inspired me and reminded me how deeply music can connect with emotions. Outside of digital stuff I’m really into collecting figurines, especially from anime and games I admire. It’s one of the ways I stay close to the characters and stories that inspire me the most. Whether I’m coding late at night or setting up my next display shelf, I’m always working on turning my passions into something I can share. Thanks for visiting I’m excited for what comes next.
